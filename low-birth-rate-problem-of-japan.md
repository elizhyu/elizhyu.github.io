<div style="position:absolute;left:72.00px;top:162.00px" class="cls_003"><span class="cls_003">which means that even though its economy is still growing, it’s slowing down. As a result, most</span></div>
<div style="position:absolute;left:72.00px;top:189.60px" class="cls_003"><span class="cls_003">young generation of Japan, who were born in a rich environment with fast-growing economy,</span></div>
<div style="position:absolute;left:72.00px;top:217.20px" class="cls_003"><span class="cls_003">don’t think they can achieve better life quality when they start living by their own. The lack of</span></div>
<div style="position:absolute;left:72.00px;top:244.80px" class="cls_003"><span class="cls_003">financial confidence makes them less likely to consider marriage rather than maintaining life</span></div>
<div style="position:absolute;left:72.00px;top:272.40px" class="cls_003"><span class="cls_003">quality. Secondly, the economic bubble happened in the 90s and the several natural disasters</span></div>
<div style="position:absolute;left:72.00px;top:300.00px" class="cls_003"><span class="cls_003">happened after 2000 gave another punch on the face of Japan economy. Even decades after,</span></div>
<div style="position:absolute;left:72.00px;top:327.48px" class="cls_003"><span class="cls_003">Japan is still working on recovering its economy today. So most young people are more</span></div>
<div style="position:absolute;left:72.00px;top:355.08px" class="cls_003"><span class="cls_003">interested in working on a better future rather than marrying early. Third, Japan has World’s</span></div>
<div style="position:absolute;left:72.00px;top:382.68px" class="cls_003"><span class="cls_003">second largest urban to rural population ratio right after Great Britain, another island country.</span></div>
<div style="position:absolute;left:72.00px;top:410.28px" class="cls_003"><span class="cls_003">With a urban population taking 78% of the total population, Japan has a serious problem of high</span></div>
<div style="position:absolute;left:72.00px;top:437.88px" class="cls_003"><span class="cls_003">city life cost and low income in contrast. While income is already insufficient to support a better</span></div>
<div style="position:absolute;left:72.00px;top:465.48px" class="cls_003"><span class="cls_003">life in cities, young people living in cities are definitely less likely to get married or give birth,</span></div>
<div style="position:absolute;left:72.00px;top:493.08px" class="cls_003"><span class="cls_003">which are both going to add extra burden on living cost.</span></div>
<div style="position:absolute;left:108.00px;top:528.72px" class="cls_006"><span class="cls_006">Work Stress. </span><span class="cls_003">Due to both Japan’s traditional “Artisan Spirit”, which promotes hard work</span></div>
<div style="position:absolute;left:72.00px;top:556.32px" class="cls_003"><span class="cls_003">for better service or product, and economic recession, Japan is facing World’s highest rate of</span></div>
<div style="position:absolute;left:72.00px;top:583.92px" class="cls_003"><span class="cls_003">overwork and highest rate of death from overwork. Work stress is such a universal problem in</span></div>
<div style="position:absolute;left:72.00px;top:611.52px" class="cls_003"><span class="cls_003">Japan that people are less likely to consider giving birth no matter since they are already</span></div>
<div style="position:absolute;left:72.00px;top:639.12px" class="cls_003"><span class="cls_003">exhausted after work or since a newborn would add even more stress both in life and cost.</span></div>
<div style="position:absolute;left:108.00px;top:674.76px" class="cls_003"><span class="cls_003">Japan has a tradition of after-work drinking. It’s mainly office workers go drinking at</span></div>
<div style="position:absolute;left:72.00px;top:702.36px" class="cls_003"><span class="cls_003">bars with co-workers and sometimes with boss after hard work of all day. The bar activity</span></div>
</div>
<div style="position:absolute;left:50%;margin-left:-306px;top:5614px;width:612px;height:792px;border-style:outset;overflow:hidden">
<div style="position:absolute;left:72.00px;top:42.60px" class="cls_002"><span class="cls_002">LOW BIRTH RATE PROBLEM OF JAPAN</span></div>
<div style="position:absolute;left:533.88px;top:42.60px" class="cls_002"><span class="cls_002">8</span></div>
<div style="position:absolute;left:72.00px;top:71.16px" class="cls_003"><span class="cls_003">usually lasts till late in night and people usually get pretty drunk to relieve the pressure from day</span></div>
<div style="position:absolute;left:72.00px;top:98.76px" class="cls_003"><span class="cls_003">work. Thus, you cannot expect a tired worker who comes back home really late in night and even</span></div>
<div style="position:absolute;left:72.00px;top:126.36px" class="cls_003"><span class="cls_003">drunk to still struggle on giving birth.</span></div>
<div style="position:absolute;left:108.00px;top:162.00px" class="cls_006"><span class="cls_006">Office Lady. </span><span class="cls_003">Talking about office ladies, what comes to most people’s mind first is their</span></div>
<div style="position:absolute;left:72.00px;top:189.60px" class="cls_003"><span class="cls_003">attractive appearance instead of their difficulty at work. Regardless of the discrimination</span></div>
<div style="position:absolute;left:72.00px;top:217.20px" class="cls_003"><span class="cls_003">Japanese female workers are facing, for those who want to reach higher position, they would lose</span></div>
<div style="position:absolute;left:72.00px;top:244.80px" class="cls_003"><span class="cls_003">their chance of promotion in most companies once if they get married. The reason behind is that</span></div>
<div style="position:absolute;left:72.00px;top:272.40px" class="cls_003"><span class="cls_003">most companies are worrying about putting too much responsibility on a married woman who</span></div>
<div style="position:absolute;left:72.00px;top:300.00px" class="cls_003"><span class="cls_003">can take test at any time once pregnant. Some companies even refuse to hire unmarried women</span></div>
<div style="position:absolute;left:72.00px;top:327.48px" class="cls_003"><span class="cls_003">since it’s possible for them to get married and get pregnant shortly after. Such latent rule stops</span></div>
<div style="position:absolute;left:72.00px;top:355.08px" class="cls_003"><span class="cls_003">lots of women who want to have their own job from marrying or giving birth. Many of those are</span></div>
<div style="position:absolute;left:72.00px;top:382.68px" class="cls_003"><span class="cls_003">willing to or have to give promises to their companies for promotion opportunities.</span></div>
<div style="position:absolute;left:72.00px;top:418.32px" class="cls_006"><span class="cls_006">Political Causes</span></div>
<div style="position:absolute;left:108.00px;top:453.96px" class="cls_006"><span class="cls_006">Coerciveness.</span><span class="cls_003"> In Japan, marriage and reproduction are both private things that even if</span></div>
<div style="position:absolute;left:72.00px;top:481.56px" class="cls_003"><span class="cls_003">government have policies on them, they can not be mandatory. Instead, Japanese government</span></div>
<div style="position:absolute;left:72.00px;top:509.16px" class="cls_003"><span class="cls_003">only has the right to encourage people to marry or reproduce to solve the low birth rate problem</span></div>
<div style="position:absolute;left:72.00px;top:536.76px" class="cls_003"><span class="cls_003">rather than forcing.</span></div>
<div style="position:absolute;left:108.00px;top:572.28px" class="cls_006"><span class="cls_006">Voters.</span><span class="cls_003"> Japan is currently facing a severe aging society issue, which results in a large</span></div>
<div style="position:absolute;left:72.00px;top:599.88px" class="cls_003"><span class="cls_003">portion of aged voters among all the voters. Since that most policies established would focus on</span></div>
<div style="position:absolute;left:72.00px;top:627.48px" class="cls_003"><span class="cls_003">welfare of aged people, and candidates working on welfare of aged people would be elected. So</span></div>
<div style="position:absolute;left:72.00px;top:655.08px" class="cls_003"><span class="cls_003">that more resource of government would be put on aged people instead of young people, while</span></div>
</div>

<div style="position:absolute;left:533.88px;top:42.60px" class="cls_002"><span class="cls_002">9</span></div>
<div style="position:absolute;left:72.00px;top:71.16px" class="cls_003"><span class="cls_003">low birth rate is a huge problem of young people, and lots of problems of young people are the</span></div>
<div style="position:absolute;left:72.00px;top:98.76px" class="cls_003"><span class="cls_003">causes of low birth rate problem.</span></div>
<div style="position:absolute;left:108.00px;top:134.40px" class="cls_006"><span class="cls_006">Migration Policy. </span><span class="cls_003">Migration is a good solution to the low birth rate problem. In first</span></div>
<div style="position:absolute;left:72.00px;top:162.00px" class="cls_003"><span class="cls_003">place, it can increase the overall population of Japan and provide more newborns. Also, it</span></div>
<div style="position:absolute;left:72.00px;top:189.60px" class="cls_003"><span class="cls_003">provides labor force that can help with the labor shortage and economic recovery. However,</span></div>
<div style="position:absolute;left:72.00px;top:217.20px" class="cls_003"><span class="cls_003">Japan is a country that doesn’t welcome migration. The current law of Japan is super strict on</span></div>
<div style="position:absolute;left:72.00px;top:244.80px" class="cls_003"><span class="cls_003">migration and lots of differences are made in law between dealing with domestic people and</span></div>
<div style="position:absolute;left:72.00px;top:272.40px" class="cls_003"><span class="cls_003">migrants. So, while US is using migration to solve its population problem significantly, Japan’s</span></div>
<div style="position:absolute;left:72.00px;top:300.00px" class="cls_003"><span class="cls_003">refusal on migration just worsen its low birth rate problem.</span></div>
<div style="position:absolute;left:277.32px;top:335.52px" class="cls_006"><span class="cls_006">Conclusion</span></div>
<div style="position:absolute;left:108.00px;top:371.16px" class="cls_003"><span class="cls_003">In order to solve or at least optimize the low birth rate problem, three ways of action can</span></div>
<div style="position:absolute;left:72.00px;top:398.76px" class="cls_003"><span class="cls_003">be taken based on its causes, social, economic, and political. For social issues, nothing can be</span></div>
<div style="position:absolute;left:72.00px;top:426.36px" class="cls_003"><span class="cls_003">done about it in short term. They are rooted so deep into the society that even in long term they</span></div>
<div style="position:absolute;left:72.00px;top:453.96px" class="cls_003"><span class="cls_003">are hard to be changed, and even being changed they can easily get into another undesired state.</span></div>
<div style="position:absolute;left:72.00px;top:481.56px" class="cls_003"><span class="cls_003">For economic problems, Japan government is currently focusing on it, which has already done a</span></div>
<div style="position:absolute;left:72.00px;top:509.16px" class="cls_003"><span class="cls_003">good job on recovering the economy a lot and will have more improvements. For political</span></div>
<div style="position:absolute;left:72.00px;top:536.76px" class="cls_003"><span class="cls_003">difficulties, they are as hard to be changed as social issues, and a reckless policy can make the</span></div>
<div style="position:absolute;left:72.00px;top:564.36px" class="cls_003"><span class="cls_003">problem even worse. No matter what kind of action is taken in order to solve the low birth rate</span></div>
<div style="position:absolute;left:72.00px;top:591.96px" class="cls_003"><span class="cls_003">problem, the complexity of the causes of such problem and the consequence of reckless moves</span></div>
<div style="position:absolute;left:72.00px;top:619.56px" class="cls_003"><span class="cls_003">should always be considered thoroughly.</span></div>
