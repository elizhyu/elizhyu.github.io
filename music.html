Music Taste

I'm a music enthusiast enjoying mainly POP and ROCK music (including J-pop and K-pop). My favourite ones are those combining the national/cultural traditional/retro music style with international pop style, like Wagakki Band (和楽器バンド) that uses Japanese traditional musical instruments in pop/rock music.

Musician Me

Besides enjoying music, I'm also a hobbyist drummer/bass guitarist. I both play at home for fun and at worship in Church.
